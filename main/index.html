About the Event

VIRTUSA is an innovation fest organizing by ADITYA INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY AND MANAGEMENT for the first time. The objective of the fest is to promote creativity and entreprenurial skills among students.
ADITYA INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY AND MANAGEMENT has partnered with all the major bodies to give students a platform like VIRTUSA to showcase their skills and project they have done so far.
